We are a coffee bar which serves great local roasted coffee - at a pleasing price! The guy behind the counter will likely know what you like and how you like it (by memory - not extra sensory perception!) - and has almost a decade of barista experiance.

As a social hub, we are situated within keynsham's "only serious bike shop" and the setting pulls in the cycling community from the surrounding cities and the coffee conisours from the town where we are situated.
